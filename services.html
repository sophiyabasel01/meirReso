Our Services

Tourism

We provide you a good look of Baudha area, especially Stupa. These places have many tourist attractions such as: Stupa,few malls and Peace park where if gone hiking an early morning, a mesmerising view of sunrise can be seen. And many more to explore.

Comfort

Our staffs are very warm and welcoming and always on your service in case you need any help. Our rooms are very clean and well equipped with all the necesary things and also an attached toilet/bathroom. The food and drinks here is prepared by professional chefs in a very clean envinment and kitchen. All of this in a very affordable cost and we'd love to take care of yours.

Seminar

We provide seminar halls for any big/small events. Any kinds of events like general meeting, training, orientation, marriage, bratabaandha can be held in this hall. Our seminar hall can occupy upto 500people at a time. We also serve any kind of food n required amount for these big events with the help of our catering services. Also, we arrange customised themes for any big or small parties according to customers request.
